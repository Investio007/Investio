Update the language used in the Investio mobile application prototype.

The English must be written in **simple Grade 9 level English** so beginner investors can easily understand everything.

Avoid complex financial terms and technical jargon.

Use **short sentences and simple explanations**.

The tone should feel:

• friendly
• clear
• easy to understand
• helpful for beginners

The app should feel like a **smart teacher explaining investing in simple words**.

---

### Update AI Analysis Explanations

Old style (too complex):

"This company demonstrates strong revenue growth but faces increasing competition within the AI sector."

New style (Grade 9 English):

"This company is growing fast.
But many other companies are trying to compete in the same market."

### Update Investment Insights

### Update Risk Explanations

Old style:

"High volatility crypto asset with significant growth potential."

New style:

"This crypto can grow fast.
But the price can also go up and down a lot."

### Update AI Assistant Responses

AI responses should feel like a **friendly guide**.

"Tesla is a strong company in the electric car market.

The company is growing fast.

This means the investment could be risky if the market changes."
